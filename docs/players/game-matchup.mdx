title: Game Detail Page
description: View game details, RSVP, and see head-to-head history between two teams.
---

## Where to find it

Click any game card on your **My Games** or **Schedule** page. Instead of going to the full event page, you land on a focused game detail page for that specific matchup.

<Info>
  The game detail page is only accessible to logged-in org members.
</Info>

---

## What you'll see

### Team matchup

Both team logos (or colour circles if no logo is set) displayed with "VS" between them — a clear view of who's playing.

### Game details

- Date and time
- Court or field (if set)
- League name
- Week number

### Status badge

If the game status has changed, a badge appears at the top:
- Red **"Cancelled"** badge for cancelled games
- Amber **"Postponed"** badge for postponed games
- Final score displayed once the game is completed and confirmed

---

## RSVP

If you're a player on one of the teams and the game is upcoming, your RSVP button is shown on this page. Mark yourself as **In** or **Out** to let your captain know your attendance.

Captains see the full attendance panel on this page, showing RSVP counts for their team.

---

## Head-to-head history

When both teams have played each other before in the same league, a **Head to Head** section appears below the game details. It shows the results of previous matchups with a win/draw/loss summary for each team.

This section only appears when there's prior history between the two teams in the current league.

---

## Back button

The **← Back** link at the top of the page returns you to the schedule page you came from.
