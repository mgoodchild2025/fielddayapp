---
title: Register for an Event
description: How to sign up and pay for a league, tournament, or drop-in session.
---

## Find your event

Go to your league's Fieldday page — your organizer will have shared this link with you. It looks like `yourleague.fielddayapp.ca`. Click **Events** to see what's open for registration.

## Create an account

If it's your first time registering, you'll need to create a Fieldday account. Click **Register** on the event and you'll be prompted to sign up with your email address.

<Tip>
  Use the same email address your organizer has on file for you. If you've played with this organization before, you may already have an account — try **Sign in** first.
</Tip>

## Registration steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your event">
    Click **Register** on the event you want to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose or create a team">
    Depending on how registration is set up, you'll either join an existing team, create a new one, or register as an individual.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Confirm your name, contact information, and any other details the organizer has requested.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the waiver">
    If the event requires a waiver, read it carefully and sign by typing your name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay">
    Enter your payment details to complete registration. Fieldday uses Stripe for secure payment processing. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Confirmation

After registering, you'll receive a confirmation email with your registration details. Your name will appear on the team roster visible to your teammates.

## Registration not open yet?

Check the event page for the registration open date. You can't register before that date, but you can bookmark the page and come back.

## Event is full?

If an event is at capacity, you may be placed on a waitlist. Your organizer will contact you if a spot becomes available.
