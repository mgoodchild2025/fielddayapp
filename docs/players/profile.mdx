## Edit your profile

Click your name or avatar in the top navigation and select **Profile**, or go directly to `yourleague.fielddayapp.ca/profile`.

## What you can update

- **Name** — your display name shown on rosters and standings
- **Profile photo** — upload a photo that appears on your team roster
- **Phone number** — required to receive SMS game reminders
- **Position** — your preferred playing position (sport-specific)
- **Emergency contact** — optional, visible only to league admins

## SMS notifications

To receive SMS game reminders, make sure your phone number is saved in your profile and that your league has SMS notifications enabled. You'll receive a reminder before each of your scheduled games.

To opt out, remove your phone number from your profile or contact your league organizer.

## Your registrations

Your active league registrations and team memberships are shown on your **Dashboard** at `yourleague.fielddayapp.ca/dashboard`.
