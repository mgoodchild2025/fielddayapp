title: View Your Schedule
description: Find your upcoming games and location details.
---

## Find your schedule

Go to your league's Fieldday page and click **Schedule** in the navigation. You'll see all games for the current event, including date, time, court, and teams.

## Filter to your games

If you're signed in, your team's games are highlighted. Use the **My games** filter to show only games your team is playing.

## Game details

Each game shows:
- Date and time
- Court or field number
- Home and away team names
- Venue (if set by the organizer)

## Changes to the schedule

Your organizer may update game times or locations. Check the schedule page for the latest information — it's always up to date. If your organizer has SMS notifications enabled, you may also receive a text message when a game is rescheduled or cancelled.

## Cancelled games

Cancelled games are marked with a **Cancelled** badge in the schedule. Your organizer will communicate whether the game will be replayed.
