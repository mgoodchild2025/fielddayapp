---
title: Invite Co-Organizers
description: Add other admins and league organizers to help manage your events.
---

<Info>
  Co-organizer accounts are available on the **Pro** and **Club** plans.
</Info>

Fieldday has two admin roles you can assign to other people in your organization.

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Org Admin** | Full access — billing, branding, settings, and all events |
| **League Admin** | Create and manage events, schedules, and player rosters — but no access to billing or org settings |

## Invite someone

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Admins**
2. Click **Invite admin**
3. Enter their email address and select a role
4. Click **Send invite**

They'll receive an email with a link to set up their account. If they already have a Fieldday account, they'll be added immediately.

<Tip>
  Use **League Admin** for coaches or event coordinators who need to manage specific leagues but shouldn't have access to your billing or branding settings.
</Tip>

## Remove an admin

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Admins**
2. Find the person in the list
3. Click **Remove**

Removing an admin revokes their access immediately. Their Fieldday account is not deleted — they just lose access to your organization.
