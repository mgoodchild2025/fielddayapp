---
title: Set Up Your Branding
description: Customize your organization's logo, colours, and public-facing pages.
---

Your branding settings control how your organization looks to players visiting your public pages.

## Where to find it

**Admin → Settings → Branding**

## What you can customize

### Logo
Upload your organization's logo. It appears in the navigation bar on your public pages and in emails sent to players.

- Recommended format: PNG with a transparent background
- Recommended size: at least 200px wide

### Brand colours
Set your **primary colour** — used for buttons, links, and accents across your public pages. Enter a hex code (e.g. `#10b981`) or use the colour picker.

### Fonts
Choose heading and body fonts from the available options to match your brand style.

### Timezone
Set your organization's timezone. This affects how game times are displayed to players and in schedules.

<Tip>
  Always set your timezone before creating your first event. Game times are stored in UTC and converted using this setting — getting it wrong can show players the incorrect game time.
</Tip>

### Custom domain
On the **Club** plan, you can serve your org's public pages from your own domain (e.g. `leagues.yourclub.com`). See [Custom Domain setup](/org-admins/custom-domain) for instructions.

## Preview your changes

Changes to branding take effect immediately. Open your public org page at `yourname.fielddayapp.ca` in a new tab to preview how it looks to players.
