## Where to find it

**Admin → Settings → Billing**

## Free trial

Every new organization starts with a **15-day free trial** on their chosen plan. No credit card is required to start.

At the end of your trial, you'll need to subscribe to keep your account active. If you don't subscribe, your account will become read-only — your data is safe, but you won't be able to create new events or accept registrations.

<Tip>
  A reminder banner will appear in your admin panel when your trial has 7 days remaining. You'll also receive an email reminder 3 days before it expires.
</Tip>

## Subscribe

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Billing**
2. Choose a plan — **Starter** ($49/mo), **Pro** ($99/mo), or **Club** ($199/mo)
3. Click **Choose [plan name]**
4. Complete checkout via Stripe

Your subscription starts immediately and renews automatically each month.

## Change or cancel your plan

Once subscribed, click **Open billing portal** on the billing page. This opens the Stripe customer portal where you can:

- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- Update your payment method
- Download invoices
- Cancel your subscription

## What happens if payment fails

If a payment fails, your account status changes to **past due**. A banner will appear in your admin panel. Stripe will automatically retry the charge — you'll receive an email with a link to update your payment method if retries continue to fail.
