---
title: Connect Stripe for Payments
description: Link your Stripe account to accept online registration fees from players.
---

Fieldday uses **Stripe Connect** to process registration payments. Players pay you directly — Fieldday never touches your money.

<Info>
  You need a Stripe account to accept online payments. Stripe is free to sign up — they charge a standard processing fee per transaction (typically 2.9% + 30¢ in Canada/US). See [stripe.com/pricing](https://stripe.com/pricing) for your region.
</Info>

## Connect your Stripe account

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Payments**
2. Click **Connect with Stripe**
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe to sign in or create an account
4. Complete Stripe's onboarding — you'll need to provide:
   - Business or personal information
   - Bank account for payouts
   - Identity verification (required by Stripe)
5. Once complete, you'll be redirected back to Fieldday

When the connection is successful, the Payments page will show your account as **Connected**.

<Warning>
  Stripe's identity verification can take 1–2 business days. You can set up events in the meantime, but players won't be able to complete payment until verification is approved.
</Warning>

## How payouts work

- Stripe holds funds for a standard rolling period (typically 2 business days for established accounts)
- Payouts are deposited directly to your connected bank account
- You can view all transactions and payouts in your [Stripe dashboard](https://dashboard.stripe.com)

## Disconnect Stripe

To disconnect your Stripe account, go to **Admin → Settings → Payments** and click **Disconnect**. 

<Warning>
  Disconnecting Stripe will disable online payments for all your events. Players will see an error if they try to pay. Make sure no active registrations are in progress before disconnecting.
</Warning>
