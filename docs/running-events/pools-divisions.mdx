---
title: Pools & Divisions
description: Organize teams into pools or divisions for large events.
---

<Info>
  Pools & divisions are available on the **Pro** and **Club** plans.
</Info>

Use pools and divisions to split a large field of teams into smaller groups for round-robin or pool play, before moving to bracket playoffs.

## Create pools

1. Go to **Admin → Events → [Your event] → Divisions**
2. Click **Add pool**
3. Name the pool (e.g. "Pool A", "Pool B")
4. Assign teams to each pool by dragging them from the unassigned list

## Pool play standings

Each pool has its own standings. Standings are calculated automatically from game results within the pool.

## Moving to playoffs

After pool play, use the **Bracket** feature to set up playoff brackets. You can manually seed teams based on their pool play standings.
