## Player notifications

Players receive in-app notifications for key events in their league activity. The bell icon in the top navigation shows a red badge when there are unread notifications.

### Types of notifications

| Type | When it's sent |
|------|----------------|
| **Team join request** | A captain receives this when a player requests to join their team |
| **Game cancelled** | Both teams are notified when a game is cancelled (if Notify teams is on) |
| **Game postponed** | Both teams are notified when a game is postponed |
| **Game back on** | Both teams are notified when a cancelled or postponed game is restored |
| **Team invite** | A player receives this when a captain invites them to a team |

### Notification bell

Clicking the bell icon opens a dropdown panel showing all unread notifications. Each notification shows:
- A title and optional detail message
- A relative timestamp (e.g. "2h ago")
- An action button or link where relevant

**Team join requests** can be approved or denied directly from the notification dropdown — no need to navigate to the team management page.

Once a notification is actioned or dismissed it is removed from the unread list. Click **Mark all read** to clear all notifications at once.

## Email game reminders

Fieldday can automatically email players before their games. Each player receives one digest email per game day listing all their games for that day — not one email per game.

### Where to configure

**Admin → Settings → Notifications**

### Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Enable email reminders** | Master toggle — turns email reminders on or off for your entire organization |
| **Timing** | How far in advance to send the reminder |

### Timing options

| Option | When the email is sent |
|--------|------------------------|
| **4 hours before** | Same day as the game |
| **12 hours before** | Morning of game day |
| **24 hours before** | Night before the game (default) |
| **48 hours before** | Two days before the game |

### How it works

- Each player gets one email per game day, listing all their games that day in a single digest.
- Reminders are not sent for cancelled or postponed games.
- Players can opt out in their profile under **Notification Preferences → Email reminders**.

## SMS game reminders (pre-game)

Send automated SMS messages to players before each game. You can configure multiple reminders per game — for example, one at 2 hours out and another at 30 minutes.

<Info>
  SMS reminders require a valid phone number on the player's profile. Players must have SMS enabled in their Notification Preferences to receive texts. Reminders are only sent to opted-in players.
</Info>

<Info>
  SMS reminders require the **Pro plan** or higher.
</Info>

### Where to configure

**Admin → Settings → Notifications**

### Set up reminders

The master toggle at the top of the SMS section enables the feature for your org. Once enabled, click **Add reminder** to create your first reminder. Add as many as you need.

### Per-reminder settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Timing** | How far before the game to send this reminder. Options: 15 min, 30 min, 1 hr, 2 hr, 3 hr, 6 hr, 12 hr, 24 hr |
| **Enable toggle** | Turn individual reminders on or off without deleting them |
| **Message template** | Your custom message text (max 100 characters) |

### Message format

Messages are sent to players as:

> **[Org Name] – [League Name] · [your message] · Reply STOP to unsubscribe**

<Tip>
  Keep your message short and action-oriented. Something like "You've got a game tonight — see you on the court!" works well within the 100-character limit.
</Tip>

### How reminders are sent

When SMS reminders are enabled, the system automatically sends each configured reminder to all eligible players on both teams at the scheduled time before each game. Reminders are **not** sent for cancelled or postponed games.

## Game-Day Morning SMS

The Game-Day Morning SMS is a daily digest sent on game day (after 7 am local time) listing all of a player's games for that day in one message.

This is different from pre-game reminders — it's player-controlled, not configured by the org. No setup is needed on the admin side.

### How players enable it

Players go to their profile and turn on **Notification Preferences → Game Day SMS**. They need a phone number saved on their profile to receive it.

### What the message includes

One SMS per game day listing every game the player has that day — date, time, and court (if set) for each game.

## Announcements

For one-off messages outside of automated reminders, org admins can send email or SMS announcements directly to players via **Admin → Messages**. Use this for schedule changes, event news, or anything time-sensitive that doesn't fit the automated reminder flow.
